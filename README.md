By way of background: fraudlens.uk is the commercial onboarding website for FraudLens, 
a forensic video intelligence service purpose-built for retail cash fraud detection. 
The platform analyses existing CCTV footage to identify unregistered cash transactions;
moments where retail staff pocket cash before it reaches the register and
produces a court-ready evidence package that is timestamped, cryptographically signed, and chain-of-custody intact. 
FraudLens operates on a per-engagement model. 
The lite tier is a web-based tool through which the client defines the objects and
geographic zones the analysis will operate on. 
Browser-native video tools give the client full autonomy over their own footage: 
we process only a small sample of the file, without uploading it to our servers, 
to produce a partial report using exclusively client-side resources. 
For larger files and longer surveillance sequences, our technicians attend the client premises, 
run the analysis on-site using our own equipment, and deliver a discrete evidentiary deliverable. 
No software subscription and no camera replacement are required on the client side. 
The service is currently in active deployment with a first client, with footage under live analysis. 
The website is the sole commercial interface for prospective clients, corporate investigators, and retail chain partners.
